# Leveraged Decay Strategy: BTC/BITX and QQQ/TQQQ

## Abstract
This research evaluates long-underlying/short-leveraged ETF pair trades as a convexity-decay harvesting strategy and studies how drift, volatility, and autocorrelation regimes affect outcomes.

## Theory
Daily-reset leveraged products embed volatility drag that can create negative convexity under choppy conditions.

## Data
Pairs: BTC/BITX and QQQ/TQQQ with adjusted close daily bars.

## Strategy
Delta-aware hedge ratio methods: leverage baseline, rolling beta inverse, and rolling regression.

## Backtest Results
Outputs are generated into `reports/figures` and `reports/tables` via the CLI.

## Regime Analysis
Conditional returns and Sharpe across drift/volatility/autocorrelation bins.

## Risks and Failure Modes
Strong one-directional bull trends, rising borrow, and transaction frictions can overwhelm convexity harvest.

## Conclusion
Strategy viability is regime-dependent and requires robust risk controls and realistic cost assumptions.
